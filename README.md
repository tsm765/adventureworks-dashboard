# Adventureworks Dashboard
Power BI AdventureWorks dashboard: revenue, profit, orders, customers, returns, and geospatial sales analysis.
Built as a portfolio project to demonstrate data modeling, DAX, and business analytics skills.

### **Key Dashboard Pages**

### **1. Executive Dashboard**
Provides a high-level view of business performance. Includes the following: 
KPI cards, Weekly Revenue trends, Orders by Product Category (Accessories, Bikes, Clothing), Top 10 products with conditional formatting, Monthly stats.

### **2. Customer Detail**
Deep dive into customer demographics and purchasing behavior:
KPI cards, Orders by income and occupation, Weekly customer trend, top customer, slicer

### **3. Global Sales Map**
Geographical insight into AdventureWorks' international market:
Map displaying order volume by country, Region selector (Europe, North America, Pacific, Global), Highlights concentration of customers and order clusters

### **4. Product Detail**
Product-level performance analysis:
Gauges: Monthly Orders vs target, Revenue monthly vs target, Monthly Profit vs targets, Weekly Profit and Returns trend, Product slicer for item-level insights.
